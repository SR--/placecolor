Portrait

Portrait is a description of a particular phenomenon or a character, a sample that gives a glimpse into being of an object, a lifeform, a cultural construct. For example, portraits can describe animals, places, and colors. Characters can be human or nonhuman, living or abiotic, real or fictional. They come into relationships and participate in stories.
